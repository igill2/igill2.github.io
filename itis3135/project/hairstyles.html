Project Startup

What is the project?

  • The purpose of this website is to establish a platform where a variety of communities can promote their own business in their seperate page.
  • Users of the website will display their own page in the intended category and explain what they are selling. They will be able to have videos and images that shows the product and be able to start the company up. The sites can have a variety of different content, such as music interests, hair, nails, clothing, etc.
